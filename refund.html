Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

At Clerra, we strive to provide the best AI-powered memory experience. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may want to request a refund. This policy outlines how refunds are handled for Clerra Pro subscriptions.

1. Digital Services

Clerra Pro is a digital service provided via the iOS App Store. As such, all payments, billing, and refunds are managed directly by Apple. Clerra does not have access to your payment information or the ability to issue refunds directly.

2. App Store Refund Process

If you would like to request a refund for a Clerra Pro subscription, you must do so through Apple Support. You can generally request a refund for an App Store purchase within 90 days of the transaction.

To request a refund from Apple:

  • Sign in to reportaproblem.apple.com with your Apple ID.
  • Tap or click "I'd like to," then choose "Request a refund."
  • Choose the reason why you want a refund, then choose Next.
  • Choose the Clerra Pro subscription from the list, then choose Submit.

3. Free Trials

Clerra Pro offers a 3-day free trial for new users. This allows you to evaluate the premium features before any charges are made. You can cancel your subscription at least 24 hours before the trial ends to avoid being charged. Once the trial period ends and a charge is processed, any refund requests must follow the App Store process outlined above.

4. Cancellation

Canceling your subscription stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund for past payments. You will continue to have access to Clerra Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

5. Contact Support

If you have any questions about this policy or encounter issues with the app, please contact our support team at support.clerra@gmail.com. While we cannot process refunds directly, we are happy to help resolve any technical issues you may be facing.
